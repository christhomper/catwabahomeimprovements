Who We Are

The Owner Does the Work. Every Time.

Catawba Home Improvement LLC is a solo, owner-operated residential contracting business based in Catawba, Virginia. We serve homeowners throughout the Roanoke Valley — Roanoke, Salem, Daleville, Troutville, Catawba, Craig County, and New Castle.

When you reach out, you're talking to the owner. When we show up to do the estimate, it's the owner. When the work gets done, it's the owner with his hands on the tools — not a subcontractor or a crew you've never met.

That means everything gets done to one standard: ours. And we don't sign off on a job until we'd be comfortable leaving it in our own home.

What We Believe In

✓

✓

Responsive communication. We respond to calls and texts within a few hours during business hours. You won't be chasing us down.

✓

We stay in our lane. We take on jobs we can do well. If something is outside our scope, we'll tell you straight and point you in the right direction.
