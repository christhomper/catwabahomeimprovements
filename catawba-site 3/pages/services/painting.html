Interior Painting Services

A fresh coat of paint is one of the fastest ways to update a room — but only if it's done right. We take the time to properly prepare every surface before paint touches the wall, because prep work is what separates a lasting finish from one that chips and peels within a year.

We paint walls, ceilings, trim, baseboards, doors, and cabinets. We use quality paints (Sherwin-Williams and Benjamin Moore on request) and take care to protect your floors, furniture, and adjacent surfaces.

What's Included

Surface prep: filling nail holes, cracks, and imperfections
Sanding and priming where needed
Masking and protecting floors, trim, and adjacent surfaces
Walls — one or two coats as needed for full, even coverage
Ceilings — flat or eggshell finish
Trim, baseboards, and door casing
Interior doors (both sides)
Closets and utility rooms on request
Cabinet painting with proper primer and finish coats
Full cleanup — no paint splatters, no drips left behind

Paint Selection

You can supply your own paint or we can source it. We'll discuss sheen selection (flat, eggshell, satin, semi-gloss) based on the room and your preferences. For trim and doors, we recommend semi-gloss for durability.
