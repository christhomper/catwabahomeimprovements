Deck Repair & Carpentry Services

Decks take a beating. Between Virginia weather, UV exposure, and normal wear, boards rot, railings loosen, and stairs become unsafe. We handle all of it — from targeted repairs to complete rebuilds — with quality materials and straightforward pricing.

Every deck repair starts with a thorough inspection of the structure beneath the surface. We don't just replace the visible boards and walk away — we check joists, ledger boards, posts, and footings to make sure the whole deck is sound.

What's Included

Rotted decking board removal and replacement
Railing repair, re-fastening, and full replacement
Stair stringer and tread repair or replacement
Post and beam inspection and reinforcement
Ledger board assessment and repair
Joist and blocking repair where accessible
Hardware replacement (lag bolts, joist hangers, post caps)
Composite and pressure-treated decking installations
Full deck rebuild on existing footings
Debris haul-away and site cleanup

Materials We Work With

We work with pressure-treated pine (most common for repairs), composite decking (Trex and equivalent), and hardwoods on request. Material choices and costs are discussed up front in your estimate.

Service Area

We perform deck repair and carpentry work throughout Roanoke, Salem, Daleville, Troutville, Catawba, Craig County, and New Castle. Most jobs in this area receive a same-day or next-day response to estimate requests.
