Flooring Installation Services

New flooring transforms a room more than almost any other project. We handle the full job from demo and subfloor prep through final trim and transitions — so you don't have to coordinate multiple people to get it done.

Luxury Vinyl Plank (LVP) is our most requested floor type — durable, waterproof, and looks great in any room. We also install hardwood, laminate, and tile depending on your project needs and budget.

What's Included

Removal of existing flooring (carpet, tile, vinyl, laminate)
Subfloor inspection and repair (squeaks, soft spots, leveling)
LVP (Luxury Vinyl Plank) installation — floating or glue-down
Hardwood installation — solid or engineered
Laminate installation
Tile installation (floors — not wet walls)
Transition strips between rooms and flooring types
Baseboard removal and reinstallation (or new baseboard)
Underlayment installation where required
Debris removal and full cleanup

Flooring Types We Recommend

LVP: Best for most rooms — waterproof, durable, and easy to maintain. Ideal for kitchens, bathrooms, and high-traffic areas. Wide range of styles and price points.

Hardwood: Classic look and feel. Solid or engineered depending on subfloor conditions and location in the home.

Tile: Excellent for bathrooms, laundry, and entryways. Requires a solid, level subfloor.

We can discuss material options and provide a material allowance in your estimate so you know exactly what you're paying for before we start.
