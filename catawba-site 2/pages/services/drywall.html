Drywall Repair Services

Whether it's a doorknob hole, water damage from a leak, or a crack that's been bugging you for years — we patch, skim, texture, and leave it ready to paint. Most repairs are invisible once the work is done.

Texture matching is one of the trickier parts of drywall work, and we take it seriously. We'll match your existing texture (knockdown, orange peel, smooth, popcorn, or custom) so the repair blends in, not stands out.

What's Included

Small hole patching (doorknob holes, anchors, nails)
Medium and large hole repair with backing and mesh
Water damage assessment and section replacement
Crack repair and joint reinforcement
Texture matching (knockdown, orange peel, smooth, popcorn)
Full wall section replacement where needed
Feathering and skim coat for seamless blending
Primed and ready for paint on completion
Cleanup of all dust and debris

Note on Water Damage

If your drywall damage came from a leak, we'll assess the extent of the damage and make sure the moisture issue is addressed (or confirm it has been) before patching. Patching over active moisture leads to mold and re-damage.
